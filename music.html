How I got into Music

Ever since I was younger I grew up in a musical family. When I was 3 or 4 I began "playing" instruments. When I went to Middle School I joined the band program and chose to play trumpet. I continuted band through High School where I was in the Marching Band, Pep Band, Wind Ensebmle, and Jazz Band. I also play in the Concert Band at NKU off and on. My favorite genre of music is Classic Rock from the 80s-90s. I also enjoy some of the modern rock bands.
